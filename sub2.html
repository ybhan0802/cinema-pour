SCENE 02 / INCEPTION

KICK

02
킥

A dark, measured cocktail drawn from Inception. Irish whiskey, cold brew coffee, butterscotch, and white cacao settle beneath a quiet crown of cream foam.

“Each layer keeps its silence, until the final sip brings them into one.”

THE NARRATIVE CONCEPT

Kick follows the film’s descent through one dream into another. Irish whiskey gives the drink its gravity; cold brew coffee lends a sober bitterness, while butterscotch and white cacao soften the passage. The cream foam rests above them like a pale threshold between waking and sleep.

INGREDIENTS & PROFILE

  • Irish Whiskey
  • Butterscotch
  • White Cacao
  • Cold Brew Coffee
  • Vanilla Syrup
  • Cream Foam

SENSORY ANALYSIS

Cream foam touches the palate first, cool and restrained. Beneath it come roasted coffee and the warmth of whiskey, followed by the gentle sweetness of butterscotch and cacao. The finish is long, dark, and composed.
