01 / OUR STORY

WE TURN MOVIE MOMENTS INTO DRINKS.

Cinema is remembered through color, light, silence, and rhythm. CINEMA POUR translates those cinematic memories into four signature cocktails, each created as an experiential scene from a different film.

한 잔의 칵테일에는 하나의 영화적 시퀀스가 고스란히 담깁니다.

빛과 색, 침묵과 프레임의 움직임으로 기억되는 영화 속 상징적인 서사를 네 잔의 시그니처 칵테일 레시피로 재해석하여 완전히 새로운 감각의 흐름을 전합니다.

04 / PROFILE

THE SCENIC FORMAT

CINEMA POUR operates at the interface of modern layout framing, mixology, and narrative editing. Our work treats structural container proportions and liquid colors as concrete physical screenplays designed to trigger specific memories of cinema.

STUDIO ALIGNMENT

Formed in Seoul, South Korea. Our methodology explores the delicate relationship between design precision and sensory impact, treating every signature cocktail recipe as a tactile package design to be remembered beyond the glass.
