SCENE 01 / JOHN WICK

BABA YAGA

01
바바야가

A smoky, bittersweet bourbon cocktail inspired by John Wick. Tea-infused bourbon, bitter orange, sweet vermouth, and cocoa bitters create a controlled and relentless profile.

“Smoke first. Bitter orange second. Cocoa at the finish.”

THE NARRATIVE CONCEPT

Baba Yaga is inspired by John Wick and the mythic name whispered across its underworld. Lapsang Souchong-infused bourbon creates smoke and weight, while orange-peel-infused Campari, sweet vermouth, orange bitters, and cocoa bitters build a dark, disciplined finish.

INGREDIENTS & PROFILE

  • Lapsang Souchong Infused Bourbon
  • Orange Peel Infused Campari
  • Sweet Vermouth
  • Orange Bitters
  • Cocoa Bitters

SENSORY ANALYSIS

Smoky tea and bourbon lead the first impression. Campari and sweet vermouth add bitter orange and herbal depth, while orange and cocoa bitters leave a dry, dark finish.
