SCENE 04 / THE MATRIX

THE ONE

04
더 원

A lucid, low-ABV cocktail inspired by The Matrix. Peach, Kirsche, elderflower, kiwi, lime, and soda meet in a pale green drink of bright fruit and quiet tension.

“Clarity arrives not as an answer, but as the moment the veil is lifted.”

THE NARRATIVE CONCEPT

The One reflects the instant when illusion gives way to choice. Peach and elderflower offer a soft fragrance, while kiwi and lime bring a sharper green note. Kirsche adds a restrained depth, and soda water carries the whole into a clean, unsettled brightness.

INGREDIENTS & PROFILE

  • Peach
  • Kirsche
  • Elderflower
  • Kiwi Syrup
  • Lime Juice
  • Soda Water

SENSORY ANALYSIS

Elderflower and peach rise first on the nose. Kiwi and lime follow with a crisp acidity, while Kirsche lends a faint stone-fruit warmth beneath the surface. The finish is dry, light, and clear.
