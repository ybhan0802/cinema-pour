SCENE 03 / WARM BODIES

R

03
알

A warm and wistful rum cocktail inspired by Warm Bodies. Caramel popcorn, pineapple, lime, and grenadine are softened by a milk-washed finish, leaving a faded rose hue and a gentle sweetness.

“Where warmth returns, even the quietest heart remembers how to move.”

THE NARRATIVE CONCEPT

R takes its character from a world suspended between decay and renewal. Caramel popcorn-infused rum brings a familiar warmth, while pineapple, lime, and grenadine restore brightness and color. The milk-washed finish tempers every sharp edge, leaving the drink gentle and clear.

INGREDIENTS & PROFILE

  • Caramel Popcorn Infused Rum
  • Pineapple Juice
  • Lime Juice
  • Simple Syrup
  • Grenadine Syrup
  • Milk Washed

SENSORY ANALYSIS

The first impression is toasted caramel and ripe fruit. Lime gives the sweetness a measured lift, and grenadine leaves a restrained red-fruit note. The texture is smooth, the finish calm, with the rum’s warmth lingering beneath.
